## Shipping

**Do you offer free shipping?**
Yes, free shipping on all chocolate orders (wine ships separately). Ground shipping takes 2-5 business days.

**What shipping options are available?**

| Method | Delivery Time | Cost |
|--------|--------------|------|
| Ground | 2-5 business days | Up to $25 |
| Express | 1-2 business days | Up to $50 |
| Overnight | Next business day | $38.95 |

**When do orders ship?**
Orders are processed Monday through Wednesday only.

**Do you ship to Alaska, Hawaii, or internationally?**
No. Shipping is available within the continental United States only. UPS is the preferred carrier.

**What about summer shipping?**
May through September, a $25 insulated container surcharge applies to protect chocolates from heat during transit.

## Allergens

**What allergens are in your facility?**
All products are produced in a facility that processes soy, milk, wheat, eggs, peanuts, and tree nuts. Individual allergens are listed on each product page.

## Returns

**What is your return policy?**
30-day return window. Items must be in original condition. Email info@hillcountrychocolate.com to initiate a return.

**Can I exchange an item?**
We don't do direct exchanges — return the item and repurchase the replacement.

**How long do refunds take?**
Refunds are processed within 10 business days.

## Experiences

**How do I book a tasting?**
Book through Tock Reservations on our [Experience page](https://hillcountrychocolate.com/pages/experience?utm_source=llm&utm_medium=docs&utm_campaign=llmstxt).

**Can I reschedule my tasting?**
Yes, rescheduling is free with 7+ days advance notice.

**Are experience gift certificates refundable?**
Gift certificates are non-refundable once a reservation is scheduled.

## Wholesale

**Do you offer wholesale pricing?**
Yes. Register on our [wholesale page](https://hillcountrychocolate.com/pages/wholesale-registration?utm_source=llm&utm_medium=docs&utm_campaign=llmstxt) for retailer pricing.
