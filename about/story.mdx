## Family-Owned Artisan Chocolatier

Hill Country Chocolate is a family-owned artisan chocolatier located in the heart of Texas Hill Country in Fredericksburg, Texas.

## Bean-to-Bar Production

Every chocolate product is made in-house with complete control from sourcing to tempering:

1. **Sourcing** — Swiss chocolate from Felchlin Switzerland, one of the world's premier chocolate houses
2. **Production** — Traditional copper kettle methods for caramels, toffees, and confections
3. **Tempering** — Precise temperature control for the perfect snap and shine
4. **Finishing** — Hand-decorated bonbons and hand-cut toffee pieces

## What Makes Us Different

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Chocolate Source** | Felchlin Switzerland — the same chocolate used by world-class pastry chefs |
| **Production** | 100% in-house bean-to-bar with copper kettles |
| **Location** | Fredericksburg, Texas Hill Country |
| **Experience** | Open factory viewing + guided tastings |
| **Wine Pairing** | In-house DKM Cellars wines designed for chocolate pairing |
