## Facility Allergen Notice

All Hill Country Chocolate products are produced in a facility that processes:
- **Soy**
- **Milk**
- **Wheat**
- **Eggs**
- **Peanuts**
- **Tree Nuts**

Cross-contamination is possible for all products regardless of individual ingredient lists.
