## Free Shipping on Chocolate

All chocolate orders ship free within the continental United States. Wine ships separately with its own rates.

## Shipping Methods

| Method | Delivery Time | Cost |
|--------|--------------|------|
| **Ground** | 2-5 business days | Up to $25 |
| **Express** | 1-2 business days | Up to $50 |
| **Overnight** | Next business day | $38.95 |

## Processing Schedule

Orders are processed **Monday through Wednesday only**. Orders placed Thursday through Sunday will be processed the following Monday.

## Summer Shipping (May - September)

During warm months (May through September), a **$25 insulated container surcharge** applies to all orders. This ensures chocolates arrive in perfect condition with cold packs and insulated packaging.

## Shipping Restrictions

- **No Alaska or Hawaii** delivery
- **No international** shipping
- **UPS** is the preferred carrier
- Wine cannot ship to all states — check availability at checkout

## Carrier

UPS is used for all chocolate shipments. Tracking information is provided via email once your order ships.
