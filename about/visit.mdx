## What to Expect

When you visit, you can:
- **Shop the retail store** — Browse the full product line and purchase directly
- **Watch production** — See the bean-to-bar process through factory viewing windows (free, no booking needed)
- **Book a tasting** — Guided wine and chocolate pairing experiences (advance booking required)
