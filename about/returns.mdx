## 30-Day Return Window

Items may be returned within 30 days of delivery. Products must be in their original, unopened condition.

## How to Return

1. Email **info@hillcountrychocolate.com** to initiate your return
2. Receive return instructions and shipping label
3. Ship the item back in original packaging
4. Refund processed within **10 business days** of receipt

## Exchanges

Hill Country Chocolate does not do direct exchanges. To exchange an item:
1. Return the original item
2. Repurchase the desired replacement

## Experience Reservations

| Policy | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Rescheduling** | Free with 7+ days advance notice |
| **Cancellation** | Contact for policy details |
| **Gift certificates** | Non-refundable once a reservation is scheduled |
